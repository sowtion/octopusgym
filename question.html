Do you have in-house trainers?

Yes, we always have in-house trainers every time we are open. They are professionally trained to help you achieve your target through series of workout plans and exercises.

How often can I visit the gym?

It depends on your plan.

Walk-in / Daily plan Package: this allows you to 1 workout session only. Your stay during this session is unlimited, provided you continue your workout session.

Weekend package: you would be admitted only on Saturdays and Sundays within a 1 month duration. You would have to pay for a walk-in/daily session if you choose to come during the week i.e. Mon-Fri.

Monthly plan: Unlimited use of our facility.
